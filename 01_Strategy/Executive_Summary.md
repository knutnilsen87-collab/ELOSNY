# Executive Summary

## What is being built
Evida is a legal matter workspace focused on safe document-to-output workflows. It combines matter status, document ingestion, fact/evidence management, sourced drafting, verification, human review and audit-ready export.

## Why now
Legal teams are adopting AI-assisted workflows, but production use requires stronger source binding, review gates, audit trails and explainable verification than generic chat-style tools provide.

## Who it is for
Primary users are lawyers and legal reviewers. Secondary users are administrators, compliance stakeholders and support staff who need controlled access to matter status and artifacts.

## Why it wins
Evida treats sources, verification, review and audit as first-class product objects. The core promise is not faster text alone, but safer movement from document chaos to legally usable output.

## MVP in one paragraph
The first end-to-end product slice lets a lawyer create a matter, import documents, extract source-bound facts and evidence, draft from reviewed material, run verification, obtain human approval and export an audit-ready package. The system blocks unsafe export when required sources, review or verification gates fail.

## Biggest risks
- AI output appears more certain than the source and review state supports.
- Source references, draft versions and review approvals are not modeled early enough.
- Scope expands before the vertical matter-to-export workflow is reliable.

## Recommendation
PROCEED_WITH_SCOPE_REDUCTION: build the verified vertical workflow first, then expand modules and integrations after the core safety gates work.
